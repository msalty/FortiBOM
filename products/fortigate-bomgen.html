FortiGate appliances are sold as Hardware + Bundle SKUs (hardware + FortiCare + FortiGuard services in one part number) or as hardware-only with services added à la carte. The recommended approach for new deployments is a hardware bundle. All HA cluster members require identical licensing — the BOM will automatically multiply quantities.
Models marked Matrix are from the April 2026 Product Matrix but do not appear in the NGFW Ordering Guide bundle tables. Bundle SKU codes are derived from standard Fortinet naming patterns — verify final SKUs with Fortinet or your distributor before quoting.
